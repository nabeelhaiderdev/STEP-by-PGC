About Step

STEP- The Largest Entry Test Preparation Network in Pakistan- offers entry test preparatory courses of Medical and Dental College Admission Tests (MCAT/MDCAT), LMDCAT (Long MCAT/MDCAT), LMDCAT (with NUMS), MCAT/MDCAT (Crash Program), The Aga Khan University Admission Test, State Entry Test (Kashmir), FMDC (Federal Medical and Dental College Admission Test); Engineering College Admission Test (ECAT), LECAT (Long ECAT), FUNGAT (FAST, UET, NUST, GIKI Admission Test), SAT-I, NET (I, II, III), (LUMS) Lahore University of Management Sciences (SAT-I, S.S.E), LUMS (SAT-I, S.S.E) + NET (I, II, III), PIEAS Engineering University’s Admission Test and National Testing Service (NTS). Also offers entry test preparation of University of Central Punjab (UCP), Capital University of Science and Technology, COMSATS Institute of Information Technology and Namal College Admission Tests.
