Introduction

The MDCAT standardized test is designed to assess your command over basic concepts of Science (Physics, Biology, Chemistry, English), critical thinking and problem solving skills. MDCAT preparatory course trains the mind to make educated guesses and to maximize chances to pick correct answers. PMC (Pakistan Medical Council) is responsible for organizing the MDCAT for all the public sector medical colleges.
